Knight

Knights equip themselves carefully for combat and engage their enemies head-on, letting attacks glance off their heavy armor. They use diverse combat tactics and a large sword to lay devastating strikes on their enemies.
Wizard

Wizards demolish their foes with arcane incantations. Although they wield powerful offensive spells, mages are fragile and lightly armored, making them particularly vulnerable to close-range attacks.

Huntress

The Huntress is a master sharpshooter who excels in bringing down enemies from afar with great haste.
Assassin

Assassins operate in silence secrecy. Much like a ninja, Assassins are stealthy and quick, attacking quickly and decisively, eliminating the threat of resistance.
